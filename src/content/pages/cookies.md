# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and tracking technologies on this Website.

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance usability and site performance.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

Required for basic Website functionality.

### **Analytics Cookies**
Used to measure performance, traffic, and user interactions.

### **Preference Cookies**
Store your settings and interface preferences.

## 3. How We Use Cookies
Cookies help us:

- Improve loading speed  
- Analyze Website performance  
- Enhance the user experience  

## 4. Third-Party Cookies
Some tools (e.g., Google Analytics) may place their own cookies.  
These providers maintain their own privacy policies.

## 5. Managing Cookies
You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some Website features may not work properly.
